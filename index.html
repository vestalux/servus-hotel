Our three stílish rooms are waiting for our guest surrounded by the natural values of the Pogány valley. On top of providing quality lodging we offer many other services including bus transfer, breakfast and even discount tickets to local attracktions.

Stylish interior

If you ever think that you should quit the rut and take a pause, come and visit us. In our guesthouse the harmony of the surroundings and the style guarantees a calm and realxing environment for our guests seeking refuge from the monotony of daily work.

Activities for everyone

For those looking to spend their time actively Lengyeltóti offers many opportunities. The Csalogány swimming pool is open all year around and welcomes any guests who would like to swin a few laps. The rolling hills in the area offer a great opportunity for cyclists and ramblers to explore the nature easily. We can provide rental bikes to help explore the sites like Somogyvár, the Great Groves or the Lake Balaton.

Thermal bath

Only about 10km away from our guesthouse you can find the thermal bath of Csisztapuszta. It is well renowned for it's thermal water that has proven to have many health benefits. Guests visit the bath from all over the world to seek remedy for various conditions including joint aches, back aches, stomach and gut problems among many things.
